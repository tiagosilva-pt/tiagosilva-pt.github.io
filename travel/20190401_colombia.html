Exploring Colombia: Our Week-Long Journey in April 2019

In April 2019, we spent an incredible week in Colombia, starting in the vibrant capital city of Bogotá, where we enjoyed rich Colombian cuisine and visited the captivating Botero Museum, home to an impressive collection of art by Fernando Botero and other renowned artists.

We then traveled to the historic coastal city of Cartagena de Indias, with its colorful colonial architecture, lively plazas, and fascinating history. To wrap up our adventure, we spent serene days soaking up the sun on the beautiful beaches of Islas del Rosario, where turquoise waters and white sands offered perfect relaxation. This Colombian journey provided a beautiful blend of art, culture, history, and natural beauty, making for an unforgettable experience.
