4 Days in Argentina: Buenos Aires and the Pampas

In April 2025 we spent 4 days in Argentina, mostly in vibrant Buenos Aires where we explored the city center, the iconic El Ateneo Grand Splendid bookstore, the National Museum of Fine Arts, and the lively neighborhoods of Palermo and La Boca, as well as San Telmo Market, Casa Rosada, and Puerto Madero. We also ventured into the Argentine Pampas and visited Tigre and Las Praderas, where we toured a local winery and experienced rural life. We enjoyed an unforgettable tango show and tasted some of the best beef in the world at traditional parrillas.
