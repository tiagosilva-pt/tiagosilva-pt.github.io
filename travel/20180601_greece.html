Greek Adventures: A Week in June 2018

In June 2018, we embarked on an unforgettable week in Greece, beginning with the historic city of Athens. We explored its ancient highlights, including the awe-inspiring Acropolis, the Parthenon, and the bustling streets of Plaka, where history and modern life converge.

For the second part of our trip, we relaxed on the beautiful island of Naxos, enjoying its golden beaches, charming villages, and the laid-back island vibe. Our Greek adventure combined culture, history, and the perfect island escape, making it an unforgettable journey.
