Madrid - December 2021

Exploring Madrid: A Christmas Getaway in 2021

In December 2021, we enjoyed a quick but memorable 2-day trip to Madrid during the festive Christmas holiday season. Our visit began with a cultural tour of the renowned Reina Sofia Museum and the Prado Museum, where we admired works by famous artists such as Picasso and Goya.

We also explored the stunning Royal Palace, marveling at its grand architecture and opulent interiors. To cap off our short trip, we took a leisurely stroll through El Retiro Park, soaking in the serene beauty of this iconic Madrid landmark.

This brief but enriching visit to Madrid during the holiday season provided a wonderful mix of art, history, and festive atmosphere.
