A Festive Weekend in Munich: December 2018

In December 2018, we enjoyed a cozy weekend in Munich, immersing ourselves in the festive charm of its famous Christmas markets, where twinkling lights, holiday treats, and handmade crafts filled the air with seasonal magic. We explored a local museum to delve into Bavarian art and history, and topped off our visit with a stop at a traditional biergarten, savoring authentic German brews and hearty dishes. This weekend was a perfect blend of culture, holiday spirit, and Bavarian hospitality.
